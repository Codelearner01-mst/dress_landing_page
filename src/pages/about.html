How It Started

A Passion for Beautiful Fashion

NoamiDress was born in 2020 from a simple frustration: finding quality, stylish dresses in Ghana shouldn't be this hard. Founder Naomi started with a small collection of handpicked dresses, selling directly to friends and family through WhatsApp.

What started as a side project quickly became a movement. Word spread fast. Women loved the quality, the variety, and the personal touch of ordering straight from someone who genuinely cared about how they looked and felt.

"I want every woman who wears a NoamiDress to walk into a room and own it."

— Naomi, Founder

Today, NoamiDress serves hundreds of clients across Ghana, offering a carefully curated collection of evening gowns, cocktail dresses, summer styles, and luxury pieces. We've grown, but our mission remains the same: dress like a queen, always.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These aren't just words — they guide every dress we pick and every customer we serve.

Quality First

We hand-select every piece in our collection. If it doesn't meet our standards for fabric, fit, and finish — it doesn't make the cut.

Customer Love

Your satisfaction isn't a metric to us — it's personal. We treat every client like family, from first message to final delivery.

Inclusive Style

Fashion is for everyone. We celebrate diverse body types and stock sizes and styles that make all women feel beautiful.

Conscious Choices

We're committed to working with responsible suppliers and reducing waste in how we source and package our dresses.

Speed & Ease

Ordering should never be a hassle. WhatsApp ordering, fast delivery, and friendly support make it effortless.

Trust & Honesty

What you see is what you get. We post real photos, real prices, and stand behind every item we sell.
