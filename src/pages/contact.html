Quick Answers

Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I place an order?

It's easy! Simply browse our collection, find a dress you love, and click the "Order on WhatsApp" button. You'll be connected directly with us on WhatsApp to confirm your size, delivery address, and payment. We'll handle the rest!

Do you deliver nationwide in Ghana?

Yes! We deliver across Ghana, including Accra, Kumasi, Takoradi, Tamale, Cape Coast, and beyond. Delivery times vary by location — typically 1–3 business days for major cities.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and cash on delivery in select areas. Payment details will be shared when you confirm your order on WhatsApp.

Can I return or exchange a dress?

Yes! If you receive a dress that doesn't fit or has a defect, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange an exchange or refund. Please note that items must be unworn and in original condition.

Do you have a size guide?

Absolutely! We carry sizes XS through XXL. When you place an order on WhatsApp, just share your measurements (bust, waist, hips) and we'll recommend the best size for you. Getting the right fit is our priority!

How quickly will you respond to messages?

We respond to all WhatsApp messages within 1 hour during business hours (Mon–Sat, 8 AM–7 PM). For emails, expect a response within 24 hours. Sundays we're available on WhatsApp only.
